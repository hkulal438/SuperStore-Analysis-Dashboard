**Super Store Sales Dashboard**
This Super Store Sales Dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of sales, profit, quantity, and delivery trends across multiple regions, categories, and time periods. Designed in Power BI, the dashboard helps business teams, sales managers, and analysts understand overall business performance, track yearly growth, and identify areas of improvement. It visualizes key metrics such as total sales, profit, and average delivery time and breaks down performance through interactive charts covering payment modes, regions, segments, ship modes, categories, sub-categories, and state-wise sales distribution. With time-series visuals for sales and profit YoY, the dashboard supports data-driven decisions for improving operations and maximizing profitability.
